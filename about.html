About me

My name is Jenny Gelua, and I am delighted to introduce myself as a dedicated student pursuing a Bachelor's degree in Information Technology at Sorsogon State University (SSU) Bulan Campus. As I navigate through my academic journey, I find immense joy and fulfillment in exploring the intricate world of technology and its endless possibilities.

Beyond my academic pursuits, I have a deep-seated passion for music, particularly singing. There's something truly magical about expressing oneself through melodies and lyrics, and I often find solace and inspiration in the harmonies I create. Music serves as a constant companion in my life, guiding me through both the highs and lows with its comforting embrace.

Additionally, I have a keen interest in K-pop, a vibrant and dynamic genre that captivates audiences worldwide with its infectious energy and captivating performances. Immersing myself in the colorful world of K-pop allows me to escape reality momentarily and indulge in the creativity and artistry of talented artists.

As I continue my journey at SSU Bulan Campus, I am eager to expand my knowledge, embrace new experiences, and contribute meaningfully to the field of Information Technology. With a curious mind, a passion for learning, and a love for music and K-pop, I am excited to see where this journey takes me and the opportunities that await along the way.
